What is Catchfire?

Catchfire Electronics is a small project dedicated to fine craftsmanship in a variety of electronics areas, from custom ham radio equipment and boutique mechanical keyboards to theoretical musings and software development.

Who is Catchfire?

Catchfire is the sole endeavour of a one Ethan Kelly , a curious mind with a love of learning and a dedication to iteration.

Why is Catchfire?

Catchfire exists as my engineering outlet and a way to share and foster advancements in open source educational content and technology. This website, for example, is written using HTMX and Tailwind, with a Rust-y actix-web backend! Go to to learn more!
